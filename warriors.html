Great Warriors of Sanātan Dharma

Sanātan Dharma has given rise to countless great warriors who protected dharma, culture, and truth. Below are some of the most inspiring warriors:

  • Bhagwan Ram – The warrior king of Ayodhya who defeated Ravan.
  • Arjuna – The greatest archer of Mahabharata.
  • Bhishma Pitamah – The invincible warrior of the Kuru dynasty.
  • Karna – The generous yet tragic hero of Mahabharata.
  • Abhimanyu – The brave son of Arjuna who fought in the Chakravyuh.
  • Maharana Pratap – The Rajput king who resisted Mughal rule.
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj – The Maratha warrior king who established Swarajya.
  • Mihira Bhoja – A strong defender of Sanātan Dharma from the Gurjara-Pratihara dynasty.
  • Samudragupta – The “Napoleon of India,” known for his military conquests.
  • Swami Vivekananda – A spiritual warrior who revived Sanātan Dharma in modern times.
